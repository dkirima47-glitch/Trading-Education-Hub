📊 Technical Analysis

Technical analysis involves studying price charts and patterns to predict future market movements. Key concepts include:

  • Support & Resistance – Price levels where trends reverse
  • Moving Averages – Smooth out price data to identify trends
  • RSI (Relative Strength Index) – Measures overbought/oversold conditions
  • Candlestick Patterns – Doji, Hammer, Engulfing patterns

💰 Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis evaluates a company's financial health and economic factors:

  • Earnings Reports – Quarterly revenue and profit
  • P/E Ratio – Price to Earnings valuation metric
  • GDP Reports – Economic growth indicators
  • Interest Rates – Central bank policy impact

⚠️ Risk Management

Protect your capital with these essential rules:

  • 2% Rule – Never risk more than 2% per trade
  • Stop Losses – Automatic exit at predetermined price
  • Position Sizing – Calculate how many shares to buy
  • Risk/Reward Ratio – Aim for 1:2 or higher

🧠 Trading Psychology

Emotional discipline separates successful traders:

  • Fear & Greed – Recognize emotional triggers
  • FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) – Avoid chasing rallies
  • Revenge Trading – Don't trade after losses
  • Trading Journal – Track and review all trades
