⚡ Popular Trading Strategies

Explore different approaches to trading and find what fits your style.

📈 Trend Following

Best for: Swing traders and position traders

Concept: "The trend is your friend" – buy in uptrends, sell in downtrends.

Tools needed: Moving averages (50, 200), trendlines, ADX indicator.

Example: When 50-day MA crosses above 200-day MA ("Golden Cross"), buy signal.

🔄 Scalping

Best for: Day traders with fast execution

Concept: Make dozens of small profits on tiny price movements.

Tools needed: 1-minute charts, Level 2 data, low spreads.

Example: Buy at $10.00, sell at $10.05 – quick 0.5% profit.

🔄 Mean Reversion

Best for: Range-bound markets

Concept: Prices eventually return to their average after extreme moves.

Tools needed: Bollinger Bands, RSI, Stochastic oscillator.

Example: When RSI drops below 30 (oversold), buy expecting a bounce.

📰 News Trading

Best for: Economic event specialists

Concept: Trade based on news releases (earnings, economic data).

Tools needed: Economic calendar, news feed, fast platform.

Example: If Fed raises rates more than expected, short bonds / long USD.
